Site Reliability Engineer — Infrastructure Platform
Stripe | Remote India / Bengaluru

About the Role

Stripe's Infrastructure Platform team builds and operates the foundation that powers payments for millions of businesses worldwide. We are looking for a Site Reliability Engineer who thrives at the intersection of software engineering and operations — someone who treats infrastructure as code, automates everything worth automating, and takes pride in systems that just work.

You will own reliability for critical distributed systems, drive incident response culture, and partner directly with product engineering teams to ship reliable software faster.

Responsibilities

- Own and improve SLOs, error budgets, and alerting strategies for Stripe's core payment infrastructure
- Drive incident response end to end: detection, mitigation, post-mortem, and systemic follow-through
- Build internal platforms and tooling that reduce toil and increase engineering velocity across teams
- Design, deploy, and operate distributed systems on Kubernetes at scale across multiple availability zones
- Implement and maintain observability pipelines: metrics (Prometheus/Datadog), structured logs, and distributed tracing
- Collaborate with software engineers to bake reliability into services from design through deployment
- Contribute to GitOps-based deployment pipelines (ArgoCD, Helm) and enforce progressive delivery practices
- Perform capacity planning, load testing, and cost optimization for infrastructure running at global scale
- Participate in on-call rotations with a strong focus on reducing alert fatigue and improving runbook quality

Requirements

- 3+ years in SRE, Platform Engineering, or Infrastructure Engineering roles
- Deep Linux knowledge: system calls, networking stack, memory management, file systems
- Strong experience with Kubernetes: scheduling, autoscaling, network policies, resource management
- Proficiency in at least one of Python, Go, or Ruby for automation and tooling
- Solid understanding of distributed systems: consensus, replication, failure modes, CAP tradeoffs
- Experience designing and operating observability stacks (Prometheus, Grafana, alerting pipelines)
- Proven track record owning incident response: on-call, SEV classification, blameless post-mortems
- Comfortable working across time zones in an async-first engineering culture

Nice to Have

- Experience with Terraform or Pulumi for infrastructure as code
- Familiarity with payment systems, financial infrastructure, or high-compliance environments
- Open source contributions to reliability, observability, or infrastructure tooling
- Experience with chaos engineering or resilience testing frameworks
